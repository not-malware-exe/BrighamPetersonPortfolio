Description

This game is an mobile, 2d, turn based, arcade style game.

The premise of the game is simple, you, the player, plays as a small little creature who moves only by dashing. When you dash, your hunger bar goes down depending on how far you dash. If your hunger bar depletes, you die. You have to dash into and eat pellets that spawn in the world around you to regain hunger. But, you have to be careful, there are other creatures that are also hungry, that are bigger than you. They predict where your are about to dash, and dash when you dash. Most of them will not eat the pellets but they will try to eat you.
