Description

Snek is a 3d clone of Snake where you can move in all 6 axes of freedom.

Snek is a full game with a main menu, settings, and tutorial that was developed in under a month. Just like Snake, in Snek, you run into apples to eat them, which extends your body length. And as you play you have to avoid hitting your own body segments and the world boundary. But, when you eat enough apples, the world boundray expands its side lengths by two units, freeing up space for the game to keep going.
