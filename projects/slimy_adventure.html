Description

'Slimy Adventure' is a 2D, stealth game where the player plays as a little slime that is trapped on a monster farm and needs to escape. Escape is not easy, the farm is covered in guards who patrol the area for any escaped monsters that should be returned to their pin. The guards has a limited cone of vision that restricts where they can see and is obstructed by any solid obstacle that the player can hide behind. Along your journey, you can find allies that want to escape with you who also have abilities that can aid you in your escape, but they can also be a burden because they can also be spotted by the guards and captured. By the end of the game, the player has to escape on the boat, whether it be with their allies or not.
