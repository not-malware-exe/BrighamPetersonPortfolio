Description

Duck of Wisdom is a chat bot designed for rubber ducking.

This program is designed for programmers to be able to think through problems by chatting with a digital all knowing rubber duck that is powered by a random number generator. When a message is sent, the rubber duck plays one of three random animations that varies in rarity and responds with a random message that also varies in rariety. All chats are saved with the date the conversation is started, the conversations can be renamed and deleted.
